Inventory management software is a computer-based system for tracking inventory levels, orders, sales and deliveries.It can also be used in the marketing industry to create a work order, bill of materials and other Selling-related documents.

Companies can use inventory management software to avoid product overstock and outages. It is a tool for organizing inventory data that before was generally stored in hard-copy form or in spreadsheets.

This Inventory management software is made up of several key components, all working together to create a cohesive inventory for many organizations' systems. These features include:
1.Order management.
2.Supplier management.
3.Customer Data.
4.Product identification.
5.Inventory optimization.
